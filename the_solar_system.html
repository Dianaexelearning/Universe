The Solar System

Planets are big balls of rock or gas that move around stars.

All the planets, asteroids and comets move around the Sun.This movement is called revolution. There are eight planets in the Solar System: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

The Earth takes 365 days, or one year, to complete one revolution around the Sun.
