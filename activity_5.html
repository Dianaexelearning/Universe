Activity 5

Speaking activity. Solar System model.

Follow the instructions to make your Solar System model.

  • Take the planets worksheet and write the name of the planets in one side.
  • Colour  the planets and cuts out the base and the planets. 
  • Attach the planets to the base using pieces of wool.

Now, present your Solar System model to the class. For example, respond to these questions.

Which planet is blue?

Which is your favourite planet?

Was it difficult to make?

Another Solar System model.

Do you want to make a professional Solar System model and share with your classmates? If the answer is affirmative, you can watch this video. This is an example.
