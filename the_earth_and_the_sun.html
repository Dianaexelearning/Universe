The Earth and the Sun

The Sun

The Sun is a star and it is at the centre of the Solar System. The Sun is important to us because it gives us heat and energy that allows life.





The Earth 

The Earth has a lot of water (75%) and its divided between oceans, seas, rivers and lakes. The remaining 25% is land distributed between continents and islands. The Earth colour seen from the space is blue, due to the sea water.

Because of its water, Earth is home to millions of species of plants and animals.
